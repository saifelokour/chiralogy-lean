# Contributing

There is no master copy. This is not a canonical upstream with derivative
forks. Any copy may be forked, rewritten, and re-registered: a different
natural language, a different proof assistant (Coq, Agda, Isabelle), different
notation, and none is authoritative. All are Autology iff the theorems are
preserved structurally. The invariant is the structure, not any repository or
any register (see INVARIANCE.md).

Findings are shared, not upstreamed. A finding travels with its proof, its
grade, and its counter-bias; each fork verifies it in its own register. There
is no gate because there is no gatekeeper, only the discipline, which travels
with the finding:

  - only universal ●● theorems belong in Kernel / Model / Protocol
  - a ● reading (an actual system instantiates the structure) is a Register,
    and Registers are yours: they never belong to anyone else's copy
  - #print axioms clean, no sorry, no new axioms, builds as a unit
  - a counter-bias: name why you want it universal, then distrust that

A finding that is domain-specific is a Register, not a correction to the
structure. Most exciting findings are Registers. That is the common case.

It stays open. The one prohibition, applied to the artifact, is enclosure.
Change everything except its freedom. You may not close it: a closed,
owned, complete copy is the attempt performed on the thing itself, and it is
the single move the license forbids.
