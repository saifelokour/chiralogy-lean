# The dependency structure, measured

The proof-level dependency graph (`depgraph-proof.json`, 557 nodes, 943 edges) was measured
agnostically with standard tools (automorphism by color refinement, betweenness and pagerank
centrality, Louvain community detection). The question was whether the framework's structure is
chiral in the way its content is. The measurement answers: the structure recovers the framework's
own prose self-description, and refutes the romantic version of it.

## What it is

Two structural absences are the base. `no_reflexive_object` and `hole_uniform` are central by every
measure (in-degree, betweenness, pagerank), and `fixedPoint_of_surjection`, the diagonal argument,
is the taproot all apophatic mass drains into. Everything rests on the kernel of absences, as the
prose says.

The two hands are real. Emergent communities align with character (apophatic / cataphatic) at 0.82
purity: the two hands are not an imposed label but a structural fact, and the apophatic side is
larger and more central. The structure is lopsided, with no cataphatic kernel, exactly as claimed.

The shape is a shallow, wide, layered, acyclic hierarchy. Longest path 5 edges, 81 percent of nodes
at depth 0 or 1, no cycles, reciprocity zero. A keystone fanout, not a deep chain and not a fold.

## What it is not

There is no symmetric fold. No automorphism swaps apophatic and cataphatic; the skeleton is a pure
acyclic DAG with no mirror. The romantic "symmetric empty-centered chiasm" is not present.

The empty center is peripheral. `center_is_empty` ranks 73rd by in-degree and 83rd by pagerank: it
is not a hub and not a center. It is empty of structural weight, which is the honest meaning of
empty. The empty center is a content keystone, a term proofs invoke, not a structural one; the
structural keystone is the pair of absences it is a corollary of. Self-application lives in the
content and in the kernel-as-base, not in any cycle or fold of the skeleton.

## Two things no semantic axis named

The framework's only real symmetry is instance interchange. The six registers, the six "buys"
theorems, and the six borrowed fillings are structurally identical (automorphism orbits): parallel
arms, uniform to the point of symmetry. This is a proper symmetry of repeated instances, not the
mirror of two hands.

`fixedPoint_of_surjection`, the diagonal argument, is the single deepest root (top pagerank), below
even the two absences it produces. No stratum or character tag flagged it; the measurement did.

## The graph adjudicated our vocabulary

Community structure aligns with stratum (purity 0.87) and character (0.82) but not with modality
(0.57) or the intra / inter axis (0.66). So stratum and character are structural, carved at the
joints; modality and intra / inter are lenses we imposed on a differently shaped structure. The
graph judged its own descriptors and kept two of four.

Full structure is in `depgraph-proof.json`; the self-image render (`depgraph-proof.svg`) draws it:
the absences and the taproot at the base, the two hands as colour masses, the empty center at the
periphery where it structurally sits.
